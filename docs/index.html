How it works

⚖️
WSJF Scoring

Every task gets four scores: Value, Time Criticality, Risk/Opportunity, and Job Size. The formula is (Value + Time + Risk) ÷ Size.

🌊
Domino Effect

A small task that unblocks three big ones ranks higher than any of them alone. Adjusted WSJF captures this upstream value.

🗺️
Critical Path

The longest chain of dependent tasks. Any delay on this path delays everything. These get a ★ marker.
